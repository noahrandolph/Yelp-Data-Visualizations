About our Project

Our project utilizes Yelp data (from Kaggle) and population data (from the Census Bureau) to help entrepreneurs find optimal opportunities and locations to start businesses. We provide several sets of tools to help entrepreneurs understand the competition, pick a business with lots of promise, and find the right neighborhood.

The tools are:

  1. The Business Environment
    1. Star Ratings: See how popular each business type is by:
      1. Star ratings
      2. Number of reviews
    2. Category Bubbles
      1. See how many businesses of each type are in each city
  2. Exploring Locations
    1. Business Locations:
      1. View business locations by type and city
      2. See business popularity by the number of reviews
      3. Understand business distributions relative to population density
    2. Star Distribution:
      1. How many highly reviewed businesses of each type are in each city?
  3. The Business Recommendation Tool
    1. See each business on the map based on each category- by star ratings
    2. The tool highlights commercial areas that may be good locations for a new business
      1. Identifies areas with no businesses of that type (no competition!)
      2. Identifies areas with businesses of that type (piggyback on the competition!)
